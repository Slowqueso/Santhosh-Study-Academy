About Us

Santhosh Study Academy is the best, comfortable & peaceful reading hall located in the heart of Chennai in Mylapore offering study spaces to NEET, FMGE, UPSC, CA, CMA, CS, TNPSC and other competitive exam aspirants. We provide study hall with fully air-conditioned space, fixed individual cabin with attached private locker, cabin light and plug point, free unlimited high speed WI-FI facilities. We have open terrace for you to eat discuss and relax available at any time. Students are provided with serene ambiance for their exam preparation with nominal cost. Santhosh Study Academy is the best study hall for longer hours study with utmost privacy. We facilitates students to enhance their quality of study. Our vision is to ensure a cool, conducive and pleasing environment to its aspirants.
